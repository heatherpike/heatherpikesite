About

Analytical, creative, and curious full-stack software engineer and MBA who became curious about code after working alongside devs as a digital marketing and brand manager.

After years of curiosity about coding (dabbling with the occasional CS or web design course over the years and inspecting the code I didn't have access to edit as a marketer) and feeling like there was more to my career path than marketing, I finally went down the rabbit hole and fell in love. I recently completed an immersive software engineering program at Fullstack Academy of Code and am excited to begin my new career as a developer.

Skilled in the JavaScript MEAN stack (MongoDB, ExpressJS, AngularJS, and Node.js). Knowledgable about RESTful web services and unit testing. Sticking my fingers in websockets to build fun, interactive apps. Learning the magic of REACT and exploring the beauty of Python and Ruby.

I'm seeking a software development position in the San Francisco Bay Area where I can leverage both my coding acumen and strong understanding of marketing to work with awesome people and build innovative applications.

When I'm not coding I love trying new restaurants, cooking healthy meals, traveling, exploring the city, working out, spending time outside, and shopping.
